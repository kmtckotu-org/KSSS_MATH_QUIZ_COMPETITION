General Rules & Conduct

  • Each class sends ONLY TWO participants.
  • All participants must wear school uniforms.
  • No communication with the audience — failure results in a 5-point deduction.
  • Only simple calculators permitted (no FX/Scientific).
  • Participants must arrive by 11:18 AM for an 11:20 AM start.

Scoring System

1st Attempt (2 mins) +5 Points
2nd Attempt (2 mins) +3 Points
Bonus (Passed Question) +2 Points

Opponents have 45 seconds to answer a passed question.
